About Me

Hi, I'm Kelli Kirk, 32 years young, and I'm currently immersed in the world of code instead of administering first aid. My academic journey began at Elva Gymnasium, and I had a brief stint thinking I'd become a nurse after finishing Tartu Health Care College. But, plot twist—I swapped bandages for code, and now I'm studying to become a junior software developer at Tartu Vocational College.

I guess I realized I prefer debugging to bedside care. Programming is like magic, but without magic wands. I love figuring out how websites and apps work—honestly, it's way more exciting than trying to explain why a band-aid isn't the solution for everything.

When I'm not immersed in code, you'll likely find me baking something delicious, editing photos, writing one of the hundreds of stories swirling in my head, or indulging in an unhealthy amount of TV shows.

As for my professional accomplishments? Oh, they're definitely still a work in progress—so stay tuned.
